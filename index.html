How It Works

Send me a friend request on Discord. If I’m available (which is usually the case), I’ll accept it and wait you to share a detailed description of your commission. After reviewing your request, I’ll conduct a feasibility assessment to determine whether the project is achievable based on your requirements. During this process, I may ask a few clarifying questions. If everything looks good, I’ll be happy to take on the project free of charge for now, as I’m currently in a learning and growth phase.

Commissions

I prefer payment in Robux, and pricing is open to discussion. I provide video demonstrations of the product before final delivery (or a test game if the project is too advanced). Once payment is completed, the final product will be delivered promptly.
